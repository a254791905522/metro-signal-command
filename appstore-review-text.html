Metro Signal Command
Dispatch the midnight metro
Run the midnight metro across 50 world cities. Accelerate, brake, dodge speed limits, and resolve faults. 1-4 trains, 1372 real stations. Fully offline.
Metro Signal Command is an offline metro train dispatch puzzle for iPhone.

You are the night-shift dispatcher for 50 world cities. From London Circle to Tokyo Ginza, from NYC 1 Train to Sydney Metro, you control the trains through the midnight low-traffic window. Keep them on time, respect speed limits, avoid collisions, and respond to faults.

HOW TO PLAY
- Tap a train to select it (yellow highlight). The first train is auto-selected at level start.
- Tap the green + button to accelerate (+0.05 per tap, up to 2x base speed).
- Tap the red brake button to decelerate (-0.05 per tap, down to 0.01).
- Watch for speed-limit zones (yellow = warning, red = enforced). Trains auto-brake and lose 8 points if speeding; +2 points per second of safe passage (combo).
- Manage multiple trains on the same line. If two trains get too close (under safe following distance), the rear train auto-brakes and loses 10 points.
- When a fault hits (signal, door, crowd), tap Resolve Fault quickly — faster resolution means higher score.
- Survive 60 seconds per level. Reach the star threshold (3 stars at 80, 2 stars at 60, 1 star at 40) to unlock the next city.

50 WORLD CITIES
- Europe (L1-L15): London, Paris, Berlin, Moscow, Rome, Madrid, Lisbon, Athens, Warsaw, Prague, Vienna, Amsterdam, Stockholm, Oslo, Copenhagen
- Asia (L16-L30): Istanbul, Tehran, Dubai, Riyadh, Karachi, Delhi, Bangkok, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, Jakarta, Manila, Seoul, Tokyo, Beijing, Tel Aviv
- Africa (L31-L36): Cairo, Lagos, Nairobi, Johannesburg, Casablanca, Addis Ababa
- North America (L37-L44): New York, Toronto, Mexico City, Los Angeles, Chicago, Miami, Vancouver, San Francisco
- South America + Oceania (L45-L50): Sao Paulo, Buenos Aires, Santiago, Lima, Bogota, Sydney

SCALING DIFFICULTY
Trains per level grow from 1 (L1-L10) to 2 (L11-L30), 3 (L31-L40), and 4 (L41-L50). Speed limits tighten. Faults appear at L10, L20, L30, L40, and L50. 1372 real stations across all 50 lines.

SCORING
Each level is scored on four dimensions: punctuality (40%), fault response (30%), passenger satisfaction (30%), plus a combo bonus from sustained safe speed-limit driving. Total caps at 100.

FULLY OFFLINE
Metro Signal Command is completely offline. No ads, no in-app purchases, no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no tracking. Your progress (highest unlocked level plus best stars per level) is saved only on your device.

KEY FEATURES
- 50 real-world metro lines with real station names (1372 stations total).
- 1-4 trains per level with staggered dispatch and following-distance collision avoidance.
- 3 fault types: signal (speed cap), door (longer dwell), crowd (extra dwell).
- Speed-limit zones with combo scoring for safe passage.
- 4-dimension scoring: punctuality, fault response, satisfaction, bonus.
- 3-star rating per level; progress saved locally.
- Portrait orientation, iPhone only, suitable for all ages (4+).

Review Notes for Apple

Metro Signal Command is a fully offline single-player puzzle game. There are no accounts, no network calls, no ads, and no in-app purchases. To play: tap a train to select it (the first train is auto-selected at level start), use the green + button (bottom-right) to accelerate and the red brake button (bottom-left) to decelerate, then keep trains on time through the 60-second shift. Avoid speed-limit zones while speeding and keep trains apart to avoid auto-brake penalties. When a fault appears (signal, door, or crowd), tap the Resolve Fault button in the center of the screen. Reach a score of 40 to clear the level and unlock the next city.
