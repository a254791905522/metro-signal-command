Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 27, 2026

Metro Signal Command is an offline single-player metro train dispatch puzzle game. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Information Collection

Metro Signal Command does not collect personal information. The app does not include account registration, social login, advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party purchase services.

Local Game Data

The app stores gameplay progress locally on your device: the highest unlocked level and the best star rating (0-3) for each cleared level, plus music and sound effect toggle preferences. This information stays on your device and is not sent to us or any third party.

Children's Privacy

Metro Signal Command is suitable for all ages. Because the app does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

Internet, Ads, and Purchases

  • No internet connection is required for gameplay.
  • No advertisements are included.
  • No in-app purchases are included.
  • No user tracking is included.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published with a new effective date.

Contact

After release, you can contact support through the Metro Signal Command App Store product page.
